Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles: 12 noodles (cooked according to package directions)
  • Ground Beef: 1 pound
  • Italian Sausage: 1 pound (optional, or replace with more ground beef)
  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Crushed Tomatoes: 2 cans (28 ounces each)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 cans (6 ounces each)
  • Tomato Sauce: 2 cans (15 ounces each)
  • Ricotta Cheese: 16 ounces
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 16 ounces, shredded
  • Parmesan Cheese: 3/4 cup, grated
  • Fresh Parsley: 1/4 cup, chopped (or 1 tablespoon dried parsley)
  • Fresh Basil: 1/4 cup, chopped (or 1 tablespoon dried basil)
  • Italian Seasoning: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook 12 lasagna noodles according to the package directions, then drain and set them aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook 1 pound of ground beef and 1 pound of Italian sausage (optional) over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add 1 large finely chopped onion and 4 minced garlic cloves to the skillet, cooking until the onion is soft.
  5. Stir in 2 cans (28 ounces each) of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans (6 ounces each) of tomato paste, and 2 cans (15 ounces each) of tomato sauce. Add 1/2 cup of water.
  6. Season the mixture with 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil (or 1 tablespoon dried basil), 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried parsley), 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon fennel seeds (optional), 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper. Simmer for at least 30 minutes.
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine 16 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 large egg, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried parsley), and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  8. Spread a layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  10. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  11. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese (about 16 ounces) over the ricotta mixture.
  12. Repeat the layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce topped with shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese (about 3/4 cup).
  13. Cover the dish with foil (spray the foil with cooking spray to prevent sticking).
  14. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  15. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
