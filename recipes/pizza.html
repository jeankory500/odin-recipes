Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce (or Pizza Sauce)
  • Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)

Topping(optional)

  • Pepperoni
  • Ham
  • Bacon
  • Pineapple

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough by mixing 2 1/2 cups of flour, 1 cup of warm water (about 110째F or 45째C), 1 packet of active dry yeast (2 1/4 teaspoons), 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth, then let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  5. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce (or pizza sauce) evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  7. Add any optional toppings, such as sliced pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or olives.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired and serve. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
