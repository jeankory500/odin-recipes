Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread: Slices of your choice (white, whole wheat, rye, etc.)
  • Protein: Options include deli meats (turkey, ham, roast beef), grilled chicken, tuna salad, or a vegetarian option like tofu or hummus.
  • Cheese: Slices of your choice (cheddar, Swiss, American, provolone, etc.)
  • Condiments: Mustard, mayonnaise, ketchup, or other spreads
  • Vegetables: Lettuce, tomato slices, cucumber, onion, pickles
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients and place them on a clean workspace.
  2. Lay out two slices of bread on a plate or cutting board.
  3. Using a butter knife, evenly spread your choice of condiments (mustard, mayonnaise, etc.) on one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Take your chosen protein (deli meats, grilled chicken, etc.) and layer it on one slice of bread.
  5. Place one or two slices of cheese on top of the protein.
  6. Layer on your vegetables (lettuce, tomato slices, cucumber, onion, pickles) over the cheese.
  7. Add a pinch of salt and pepper if desired.
  8. Place the second slice of bread, condiment side down, on top of the stacked ingredients to complete the sandwich.
  9. Using a sharp knife, cut the sandwich in half diagonally or vertically if you prefer.
  10. Place the sandwich on a plate and enjoy!
